# IPL-Analysis-

## Problem Statement:

"Sports Basics" is a sports blog company that entered space recently. They wanted to get more traffic to their website by releasing a special edition magazine on IPL 2024. This magazine aims to provide interesting insights and facts for fans, analysts and teams based on the last 3 year's data.

## Overview:

As a aspirant data analyst, I have analyzed three years of IPL data to create a comprehensive report using Power BI. The report includes primary insights such as Team analysis, batters analysis, bowlers analysis, and secondary analysis covering best 11 players, top 3 all-rounders , 2024 qualifiers prediction, winner and runner-up prediction, orange cap, and purple cap prediction.

## 🔍Key Objectives:

**▫️**Analyzed teams and matches.

**▫️**Calculation of different cricket metrics

**▫️**Provide insights based on performance of players

**▫️**Provide predictions for 2024 based on analysis and current performance of teams and players.
